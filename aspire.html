About aspire 2017

The Aspire Conference is an opportunity for young people such as students, professionals, researchers and entrepreneurs e.t.c committed to innovations, entrepreneurship development to meet, learn, network and partake in creating abundance. It also afford business and public officers to gain insightful solutions to strategic problems in an open innovation forum.

Join us in Lagos/Ekiti, the most populous city and hub of creativity in Africa or the fountain of knowledge and beautiful landscape with several untapped opportunities and talents.

As A Professional/Entreperneur

From having a bite at where the world is heading, to understanding exponential innovation and the economics of innovation: leading within a global environment to leading changes, planning within scenarios and managing projects, you also get to have a bite at managing co-creation , open innovation and leading a value-oriented firm.
